About Us

ELLEVA Talent is a premier modeling and talent agency dedicated to discovering, nurturing, and elevating exceptional talent in the fashion, commercial, and digital industries. With a strong emphasis on diversity, innovation, and professionalism, we connect world-class models and influencers with top brands, designers, and creative projects.
From runway and editorial shoots to influencer marketing and digital campaigns, ELLEVA Talent provides a seamless platform for talent to shine. Whether you're a brand seeking the perfect representation or a model looking to take your career to the next level, ELLEVA is where elegance meets excellence.
